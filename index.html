Top 5 Study Tips

🎯 Set Clear Goals

Write down what you want to achieve in each study session.

📖 Use Active Learning

Summarize notes, explain concepts aloud, and practice with questions.

⏳ Follow Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break, and repeat.

📝 Create a Study Schedule

Allocate time for each subject and stick to a routine.

🌿 Stay Organized

Keep your workspace clean and distraction-free.

💡 Why Study Smart?

  • ✅ Improves focus and concentration
  • ✅ Saves time and reduces stress
  • ✅ Helps retain information effectively
  • ✅ Makes learning enjoyable and interactive
